Prepare the following testing paraphernalia:

1. First Aid Kit

2. Drinking Water and a small towel or bimpo

3. Individual score cards

4. During testing:

  • Body Composition- tape measure, weighing or bathroom scale, L-square or ruler.
  • Flexibility- tape measure
  • Cardiovascular Endurance- stopwatch, step box/stairs
  • Muscular Strength- mat
  • Speed- stopwatch
  • Power- meter stick/tape measure
  • Agility- tape measure, masking tape/chalk,
  • Reaction Time- plastic ruler (12 inches)
  • Coordination- sipa (washer weighing 4 gms with straw)/20 pcs. bundled rubber bands/any similar local materials weighing 4 gms.
  • Balance- stopwatch
