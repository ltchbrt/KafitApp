40 METER SPRINT

- to measure running speed.

EQUIPMENT

Stopwatch.

Running area with known measurement (40 meters)

Procedure

For the Performer:

* At the signal "Ready", stand behind the take-offline, the tips of the shoes should not go beyond the line and assume a crouch position.

* At the signal "Get Set", assume an un-crouch position (buttocks up) with both hands on the starting line.

* At the signal "GO", run to the finish line as fast as you can.

For the Partner:

* Set the stopwatch to zero (0) point.

* At the signal "GO" start the watch and stop it as the performer crosses the finish line.

* Record time in the nearest 0:00:01 seconds

SCORING

* Record the time in nearest minutes and seconds.
